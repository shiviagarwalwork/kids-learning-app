# Privacy Policy for Unicorn Learning

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Unicorn Learning ("the App") is a children's educational application designed for kids ages 3-6. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation for children (GDPR-K).

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information from children.**

### Data Stored Locally on Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

- **Learning Progress:** Game scores, stars earned, and learning statistics to track educational progress
- **App Preferences:** Settings such as sound/music toggles and voice preferences
- **Adaptive Learning Data:** Performance tracking for letters, numbers, and math facts to personalize the learning experience

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Can be deleted at any time through the app's Settings

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Names or personal identifiers
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Photos or videos
- Voice recordings
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Any data that could identify a child

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- External APIs that collect user data

## Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- Review the learning data stored on the device
- Delete all app data through the Settings screen
- Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

## Data Security

All learning data is stored locally using encrypted device storage. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data breaches from our servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please contact us at:

**Email:** privacy@unicornlearning.app

## COPPA Compliance Statement

Unicorn Learning is designed for children under 13 and complies with COPPA by:
1. Not collecting personal information from children
2. Not requiring account creation
3. Not enabling social features or communications
4. Storing all data locally on the device
5. Providing parents with control over their child's data

## GDPR-K Compliance Statement

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements for children's data by:
1. Processing data only on the local device
2. Not transferring data internationally
3. Not sharing data with third parties
4. Providing easy data deletion through app Settings
